What important element is missing from the central door?


It's an element that normally allows you to open a door.

The Marseille City Hall has a particular history. During its construction under Louis XIV, the ground floor and the first floor were not connected by a staircase. The Sun King wanted to show that the royal authority, which occupied the first floor, was above the authority of the Marseille notables who occupied the ground floor. The only way to reach the first floor was through a walkway, accessible from another building.
