This little corner of Marseille, made famous by the numerous filming locations that took place here, was already a must-see in the 18th century. Indeed, the Place des 13 Coins, formerly called "Place des 13 Cantons" in reference to the inn run there by a Swiss man, was the favorite haunt of a historical figure who has become almost legendary: Casanova. It is said that this is where he liked to spend the night, looking for young Marseille women to seduce and satisfy his desire to conquer.
